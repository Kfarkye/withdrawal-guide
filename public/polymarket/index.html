Frequently Asked Questions

How do I withdraw from Polymarket?
You withdraw USDC directly to your Web3 wallet, or to a centralized exchange that supports Polygon network deposits.
What are Polymarket withdrawal fees?
Polymarket does not charge withdrawal fees. You only pay standard Polygon network gas fees, which are typically fractions of a cent.
Can I withdraw to my bank account from Polymarket?
No, you must withdraw USDC to a crypto exchange (like Coinbase) first, sell the USDC for USD, and then initiate an ACH transfer to your bank.
