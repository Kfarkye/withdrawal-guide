Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my Kalshi withdrawal locked?
Kalshi enforces a strict Security Hold on ACH transfers. You cannot withdraw funds until 2 business days AFTER the initial deposit fully clears.
Can I bet and withdraw under a corporate LLC account?
Yes. Kalshi explicitly allows institutional and corporate entity accounts. Ensure your linked bank account matches the exact LLC name.
