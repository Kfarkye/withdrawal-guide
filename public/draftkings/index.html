Frequently Asked Questions

How fast are DraftKings withdrawals?
Debit card and PayPal withdrawals typically process in under 24 hours, and often within an hour. Bank wires take up to 2 days, and physical checks can take up to 14 days.
Are there fees for withdrawing from DraftKings?
No, DraftKings does not charge fees for standard withdrawal methods like PayPal, debit cards, or online banking.
Can I withdraw my bonus bets on DraftKings?
No. Bonus bets cannot be withdrawn directly. You must wager them, and any profit you make becomes withdrawable cash.
