Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Bovada Bitcoin withdrawal take?
Bovada states Bitcoin withdrawals take up to 24 hours, but most users receive their funds in 1 to 2 hours after the request is approved.
Does Bovada charge withdrawal fees?
Cryptocurrency withdrawals are completely free. If you request a Check by Courier, the fee is $100, though they waive the fee for one non-crypto withdrawal every 90 days.
Why was my Bovada withdrawal denied?
The most common reasons for a denied payout are unverified identity (KYC not complete), trying to withdraw active bonus funds before meeting rollover requirements, or entering an invalid crypto address.
