Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Bovada Bitcoin withdrawal take?
Bovada states Bitcoin withdrawals take up to 24 hours, but most users receive their funds in 1 to 2 hours after the request is approved. Litecoin and Ethereum withdrawals typically arrive within 1 hour.
What are Bovada's weekly crypto withdrawal limits?
Litecoin has the highest weekly limit at $180,000. Bitcoin allows up to $90,000/week. Ethereum, USDT, and Bitcoin Cash are capped at $2,500/week — making them unsuitable for large cashouts.
Does Bovada charge withdrawal fees?
Cryptocurrency withdrawals are completely free from Bovada's side — you only pay blockchain network gas fees. Check by Courier costs $100, though Bovada waives the fee for one non-crypto withdrawal every 90 days.
Why was my Bovada withdrawal denied?
The most common reasons are: unverified identity (KYC not complete), attempting to withdraw active bonus funds before meeting rollover requirements, entering an invalid crypto address, or hitting the one-withdrawal-every-3-days limit.
